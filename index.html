Welcome to Our Project Pages.

This webpage is for our project in CS770 Human-Computer Interaction. The goal of the project is to build a tool that helps check the accessibility of a webpage and encourages web developers build a more accessible website.

General Information

Although there are different guidelines and laws to ensure the accessibility of a websites, there are still plenty of websites not accessible enough for visually impaired users.
